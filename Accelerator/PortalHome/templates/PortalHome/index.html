    • Welcome To CPE Junior College

    CPE, a name you can associate with trust and credibility, is a leading education service provider for commerce in Visakhapatnam. Formed in 2007 by three dynamic chartered accountants with the sole objective of providing quality education for CA, the institute has grown leaps and bounds over the years. After successfully placing Vizag on the national map in education by achieving All India Ranks in CA – CPT and CA -Inter, we started a junior college for MEC with a view to strengthen the basics and provide a platform for comprehensive education in commerce.

    We welcome you to join us and make us a part of your dream of achieving success in commerce education.
